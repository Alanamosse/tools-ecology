

GalaxyE Community
=================

The GalaxyE Community is devoted to the creation of resources for ecology research conducted through the [Galaxy Project](http://galaxyproject.org/).

* Home: <https://github.com/galaxyecology>


Support
-------

Issues regarding specific [GalaxyE repositories][repos] are addressed by opening a ticket in the issue tracker of that repository.



[repos]:https://github.com/galaxyecology
